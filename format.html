Formatting text

Paragraphs and line breaks

Users of HTML are sometimes surprised to find that HTML gives them little control over the way that a page is displayed. It should be remembered that HTML was developed as a means of marking up the structure of a document not as a way of determining its presentation. Formatting text to appear on a Web page is therefore different from formatting text to appear in a printed document.

Definitions of Seasons

Here Are The Meanings
Summer
The warmest season of the year, in the northern hemisphere and in the southern hemisphere.
Autumn
The third season of the year, when crops and fruits are gathered and leaves fall, in the northern hemisphere and in the southern hemisphere.
Winter
The coldest season of the year, in the northern hemisphereand in the southern hemisphere.
Spring
The season after winter and before summer, in which vegetation begins to appear, in the northern hemisphere and in the southern hemisphere.
